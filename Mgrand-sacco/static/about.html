About Mgrand Sacco

Our mission is to empower our members through affordable financial services

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Mgrand Sacco has grown from a small group of 50 members to a thriving financial cooperative serving over 15,000 members across Kenya.

Mgrand Sacco was established with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a financial institution that would serve the needs of ordinary Kenyans who had been overlooked by traditional banks.

Our founders recognized that many hardworking individuals were unable to access affordable financial services, which limited their ability to save, invest, and grow their businesses.

Over the years, we've remained true to our founding principles while embracing innovation and technology to better serve our members. Today, we offer a comprehensive range of financial products designed to help our members achieve their dreams.

As a member-owned cooperative, we're accountable to our members rather than external shareholders. This means we can focus on providing value to our members rather than maximizing profits.

Our Mission

To empower our members through affordable financial services that promote savings, provide credit, and foster economic growth within our community.

Our Vision

To be the leading financial cooperative in Kenya, recognized for our commitment to member satisfaction, innovation, and community development.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our history

2008

Founded with 50 members and initial capital of KES 500,000.

2010

Reached 500 members and launched our first loan product.

2013

Opened our second branch and introduced mobile banking services.

2016

Crossed 5,000 members and launched our investment products.

2019

Received the Sacco of the Year award for innovation.

2022

Reached 15,000 members and expanded to 8 branches across Kenya.
