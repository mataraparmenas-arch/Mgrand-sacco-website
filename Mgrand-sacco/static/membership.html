Membership Types

Choose the membership option that best suits your needs

Individual Membership

Perfect for individuals looking to save money and access credit facilities for personal development and investment.

  • Minimum share capital: Ksh 2,000
  • Monthly contribution: Ksh 500+
  • Access to all loan products

Group Membership

Ideal for chamas, investment groups, and community organizations looking for collective financial solutions.

  • Minimum share capital: Ksh 10,000
  • Monthly contribution: Ksh 2,000+
  • Group loan facilities available

Corporate Membership

Designed for businesses and organizations seeking financial solutions for their employees and operations.

  • Minimum share capital: Ksh 50,000
  • Monthly contribution: Ksh 10,000+
  • Special corporate loan products

Membership Requirements

Simple requirements to join our growing community of members

For Individuals

Identification Documents

Original and copy of National ID, Passport, or Alien ID

Proof of Residence

Recent utility bill, rent agreement, or letter from local chief

Passport Photo

Two recent passport-sized photographs

KRA PIN Certificate

Copy of your KRA PIN certificate

For Groups & Corporates

Registration Documents

Certificate of registration, constitution, and minutes appointing signatories

Identification for All Members

Copies of ID documents for all group members or directors

Bank Details

Bank statement for the last 6 months and bank details

Financial Records

Audited financial statements for the previous year

How to Join

Follow these simple steps to become a member

1

Visit Our Office

Visit any of our branches with your identification documents and other required paperwork.

2

Complete Application Form

Fill out the membership application form with accurate information and submit it to our staff.

3

Pay Registration Fee

Pay the one-time registration fee of Ksh 1,000 and the minimum share capital of Ksh 2,000.

4

Account Activation

Once your application is approved and payment confirmed, your account will be activated immediately.

5

Start Saving & Borrowing

Begin contributing to your savings account and access our loan products after 6 months of consistent savings.
