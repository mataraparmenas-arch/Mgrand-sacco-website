Loan Products

We offer a variety of loan products to suit your specific financial needs

Personal Loan

Flexible personal loans to meet your immediate financial needs for personal development, emergencies, or special projects.

  • Amount: Ksh 10,000 - Ksh 500,000
  • Interest Rate: 12% p.a. reducing balance
  • Repayment Period: Up to 48 months
  • Processing Time: 3-5 working days
Requirements
  • Minimum 6 months membership
  • 3 months consecutive contributions
  • Guarantors (2)

Business Loan

Empower your business with our affordable business loans designed to help you expand, stock, or improve operations.

  • Amount: Ksh 50,000 - Ksh 2,000,000
  • Interest Rate: 14% p.a. reducing balance
  • Repayment Period: Up to 60 months
  • Processing Time: 5-7 working days
Requirements
  • Minimum 12 months membership
  • 6 months consecutive contributions
  • Business registration documents
  • Business financial statements

Development Loan

Finance your dream home or development project with our long-term development loans at competitive rates.

  • Amount: Ksh 100,000 - Ksh 5,000,000
  • Interest Rate: 13% p.a. reducing balance
  • Repayment Period: Up to 120 months
  • Processing Time: 7-14 working days
Requirements
  • Minimum 24 months membership
  • 12 months consecutive contributions
  • Title deeds or sale agreement
  • Approved architectural plans

Education Loan

Invest in your future or that of your loved ones with our affordable education loans for school fees and related expenses.

  • Amount: Ksh 20,000 - Ksh 500,000
  • Interest Rate: 10% p.a. reducing balance

  • Processing Time: 3-5 working days
Requirements
  • Minimum 6 months membership
  • 3 months consecutive contributions
  • Admission letter or fee structure

Asset Finance

Acquire your dream vehicle or other assets with our flexible asset financing options at competitive rates.

  • Amount: Ksh 100,000 - Ksh 3,000,000
  • Interest Rate: 14% p.a. reducing balance
  • Repayment Period: Up to 60 months

Loan Application Process

Simple and transparent process to access our loan facilities

1

Application

Fill out the loan application form and submit it along with the required documents at any of our branches or through our mobile app.

2

Review

Our loan officers will review your application and assess your eligibility based on your savings history and repayment capacity.

3

Approval

Once approved, you'll receive a loan offer with the terms and conditions. Review and accept the offer to proceed.

4

Disbursement

After acceptance, the loan amount will be disbursed to your account within the specified timeframe.
