About Mgrand Sacco

We are a member-owned financial cooperative dedicated to providing affordable financial services to our community

Our Mission

To empower our members through affordable financial services that promote savings, provide credit, and foster economic growth within our community.

Our Vision

To be the leading financial cooperative in Kenya, recognized for our commitment to member satisfaction, innovation, and community development.
