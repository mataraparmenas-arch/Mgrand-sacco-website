# KYC (Know Your Customer) Form

This form is required for all members to verify their identity as part of our compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Required Documents

- Copy of National ID or Passport
- Proof of residence (utility bill or tenancy agreement)
- KRA PIN certificate
- Recent passport size photo
